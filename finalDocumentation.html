Recreating the classic Snake Game

Schematics

Using Arduino's joystick, I recreated the Snake Game in P5. I used P5.js to create the graphics and audio. I used Arduino to configure the movements to the joystick. The joystick controls the snake and allows it to move. This game is normally controlled by binary arrow keys however, the joystick allows the snake to move diagonally. The greater your score, the faster your snake moves and the audio becomes.
